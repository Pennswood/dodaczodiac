Mission Statement

UDACNY dodex is a website designed to encourage users to learn about 3D printing and the advantages of 3D printers, including their ability to create customizable, unique objects. We hope to entertain users through interactions with the 3D files on the website, and spark their interest in making their own 3D objects.
