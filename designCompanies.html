Printing companies

There are many different online stores for printing and shipping services, such as Shapeways, 3d Hubs, and Materialise.

These companies usually work in two ways. You could upload your own 3D file onto the website, or you can browse work that others have done. The models can usually be customized, with the choices of different materials, colors, designs. Certain companies allow for special customizations such as adding your name onto the model. You can also set up your own store in these websites and sell the designs that you have created. The company will calculate a base price from the work that needs to be done, the dimensions, and the material needed. They would also provide services to mathematically see if the 3D file is possible. For example, certain structures are so weak and fragile that it is almost certain to break in transportation. If the model passes "inspection" by an automated program, your model would be allowed to be sold. You can set the price for the object on the website. The difference between the price you set and the base price would be your revenue per item sold.

Our dodecahedrons are currently sold at Shapeways for $30 +, dependent on your choice of sizes, materials, colors, and designs available. Materials include aluminum, stainless steel, and plastic. Designs include the western character, Chinese characters, or Chinese cartoon shapes. Colors for the plastic material includes white, red, orange, green, blue, purple, and black.
