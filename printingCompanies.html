3D printing companies in the U.S.

There are two distinctive types of 3D printing companies. The first type focuses on selling 3D printers directly to customers. The second type focuses on letting customers upload 3D files or shop for 3D models others have made. They can customize the objects to their taste, such as choosing materials, colors, and names. Then, the company will print the model and ship them to their customers.

3D printing recently has become less popular. Varieties of designs for different uses has not been vast due to less creators and the expensive cost of printing. The most famous companies, SSYS (MakerBot) and DDD (3D systems companies) were at their height at 2014, when both stocks were worth around a hundred USD per share. Now, both are worth at most, 10-20 USD per stock.

3D printing might only seem useful for luxuries, souvenirs, and things which most would consider insignificant compared to other basic necessities such as housing, yet 3D printing has been used to create part for drones, guns, chairs, bridges, and other constructions.
